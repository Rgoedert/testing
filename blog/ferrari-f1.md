## Ferrari F1 Team

The Ferrari F1 team is one of the most iconic and successful teams in Formula 1 history. With a rich legacy of innovation and performance, Ferrari has captured the hearts of fans worldwide.

### History

Founded in 1947 by Enzo Ferrari, the team has a storied history filled with triumphs and challenges. Ferrari's first Formula 1 victory came in 1951, and since then, the team has won 16 Constructors' Championships and 15 Drivers' Championships.

### Current Season

In the current season, Ferrari continues to push the boundaries of technology and performance. With a talented driver lineup and a dedicated team of engineers, Ferrari is determined to reclaim its position at the top of the F1 grid.

### Key Drivers

- **Charles Leclerc**: A rising star in Formula 1, Leclerc has shown incredible speed and skill behind the wheel of the Ferrari SF21.
- **Carlos Sainz Jr.**: Joining Ferrari in 2021, Sainz brings a wealth of experience and a competitive spirit to the team.

### Technical Innovations

Ferrari is known for its cutting-edge technology and innovative approach to car design. The SF21 features advanced aerodynamics, a powerful engine, and state-of-the-art electronics.

### Future Prospects

Looking ahead, Ferrari is committed to continuing its legacy of excellence in Formula 1. With a strong focus on research and development, the team is poised to make significant strides in the coming seasons.
